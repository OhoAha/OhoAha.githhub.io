DevSecOps
DevSecOps is a philosophy and practice of organizational software engineering that strives to unify software development (Dev), security (Sec) and operations (Ops). The key aspect of DevSecOps is the enhancement of customer results and project performance by automating, monitoring and implementing security at all phases of the software lifecycle which include: Plan, Develop, Build, Test, Release, Deploy, Operate and Monitor. Practicing DevSecOps provides observable security and quality improvements over the conventional software lifecycle and enables application security, secure deployment and secure operations.
